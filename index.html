The Exhale Podcast is a space for sharing growth in all its forms—mental, spiritual, financial, and beyond. Born from my documentary Unleash Our Stories, where I interviewed seven individuals about their mental health journeys, this podcast continues the mission of connecting through shared experiences. After overcoming my own mental health challenges and fears, I found the courage to create this platform with the support of those around me. Each episode aims to inspire listeners to find their strength and push through life’s challenges. Together, we grow, exhale, and learn from one another.
